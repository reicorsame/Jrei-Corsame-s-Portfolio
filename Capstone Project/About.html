About The Spike Zone

What is The Spike Zone?

The Spike Zone is a webtoon inspired project created for my capstone subject. It combines my interest in web development and my passion for drawing stories.

This website shows my creativity through art, characters, and storytelling while also presenting my skills in making websites.

My Goal

My goal is to create something enjoyable, unique, and meaningful while learning more about design and coding.
